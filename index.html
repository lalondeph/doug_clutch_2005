Bio

Doug is the son of an Amish farmer and a local radio personality. Raised by his maternal method-acting grandparents, creation is in his blood. Doug began his artistic pursuits as a toddler writing on whatever he could find, with anything that would stick to it.

A late bloomer, Doug was silent until his fifth birthday. To this day he prefers to communicate through his art. Doug won his first art contest at the age of 3 and has never looked back.

This gallery is meant to showcase the modern view of Doug Clutch, as a man and an artist. It will also serve as a platform for commissions, critical feedback, and as an online store.

Philosophy

Doug Clutch is a man of few words. His bold style fits broadly into the post brutalist movement, but largely defies definition. There is no dark. There is no light. There is no Doug Clutch.

Creative Process

Doug Clutch has several creative steps that have become his recipe to success and striking imagery.

  1. Yoga
  2. Ketosis
  3. Meditation
    • Sleeping for 3-4 days
    • Staying awake for 3-4 days
    • Riding public transit during waking period
    • Walking home from end of the furthest line
    • Burning Incense
    • Drinking herbal tea
  4. Travel (mostly on foot)
  5. Shooting Photographs (mostly on film)
  6. Scanning developed photographs
